Turning Tea Waste into Biodegradable Plastic

Welcome to BIO-TEA! We're an eco-friendly startup founded by young innovators who believe in turning waste into wonders. We take tea factory waste and transform it into biodegradable plastic products that help fight pollution and protect nature.

♻️ Made from Tea Waste   🌍 100% Eco-Friendly   🧃 Strong, Safe, Sustainable

About Us

At BIO-TEA, we noticed that tea factories throw away tons of leftover tea granules. Instead of letting them go to waste, we researched how to use them to create an eco-plastic alternative. Our solution is cheap, biodegradable, and strong enough for daily use!

How It Works

✅ Step 1: Collect waste tea granules from local tea factories.
✅ Step 2: Mix with natural binders to make a strong, compostable material.
✅ Step 3: Shape into packets, containers, or plates.
✅ Step 4: Use it, throw it — and it returns to the Earth! 🌱

Our Mission

To reduce plastic waste by creating affordable, eco-friendly packaging using tea waste. We aim to spread awareness, save the planet, and inspire others to innovate sustainably.

Our Future Impact

🌿 Saving over 10,000 kg of tea waste from landfills.
🌿 Reducing plastic pollution in local areas.
🌿 Creating awareness in schools and tea-growing communities.
